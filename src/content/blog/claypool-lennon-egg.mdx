## A Sci-Fi Odyssey

This isn't just an album; it’s a high-resolution trip into a dystopian future. 
The record follows a complex narrative about an AI obsessed with efficiency and 
a quest for the "Golden Egg of Empathy." It’s the kind of conceptual ambition 
that only this duo could pull off without falling into cliché.

Musically, it’s a laboratory. You have Claypool’s signature, percussive bass 
lines—thick, elastic, and unmistakable—intertwining with Lennon’s airy, 
Beatle-esque vocals and cinematic guitar work. It feels like a 1970s sci-fi 
film directed by someone who found a lost reel in a deep-space vault.
